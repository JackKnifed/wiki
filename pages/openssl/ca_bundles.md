CA Bundles
==========

This page just includes a bunch of known good SSL CA bundles.

notice> You can determine the type of encryption of a certificate with the following command:
notice> ```bash
notice> openssl x509 -text -noout -in /path/to.crt |grep 'Signature Algorithm'
notice> ```
